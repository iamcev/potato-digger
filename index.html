POTATO DIGGER!!!

How to play: This is minesweeper. If you know how to play minesweeper, play this like you would play minesweeper.
Controls:

  • Left click: dig
  • Right click: mark
Goal: Dig up all the potatoes and don't hit the ducks!

Start by digging anywhere you want. Tiles will appear.

The number on a tile stands for how many ducks there are adjacent to it. If there is no number, good for you! The tile has no ducks next to it.

Try to use the numbers to deduce where the ducks are. If you think that a tile has a duck, mark them by right clicking the tile.

Once you clear out all the tiles without hitting any ducks, you win!
